Curiosity Rover

At the time of launch, NASA's Curiosity was the largest and most advanced rover ever sent to Mars. The car-sized rover is part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory (MSL). Curiosity's ongoing mission is to study the ancient habitability and the potential for life on Mars.

Interstellar Probe

As of 2023, there are five interstellar probes, all launched by the American space agency NASA: Voyager 1, Voyager 2, Pioneer 10, Pioneer 11 and New Horizons. Also as of 2023, Voyager 1, Voyager 2 and Pioneer 10 are the only probes to have actually reached interstellar space.
